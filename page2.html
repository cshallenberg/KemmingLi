THE MEMORY MACHINE

Please write down anything you can think of about “memory”. It could be your own memory, other’s memories, collective memory, historical memory, your philosophical ideas about “memory”, or just anything you’d like to write down. There’s no limit in length. You’ll be completely anonymous. The content you submitted will be printed out, fed into the memory machine, and made into paper bricks by the artist. The paper bricks will be used to build another installation in the future. Thank you for your participation!
